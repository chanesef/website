About Chanese A Forte

Environmental Health Sciences & Scientific Computing | The University of Michigan

Chanese is an environmental health researcher with a passion for computer science currently researching pesticide exposure and breast cancer development. Chanese's dissertation takes a hybrid approach to pesticide exposure and breast cancer risk assessment by using field research, cell culturing, predictive modeling, and big data management. In her spare time, Chanese enjoys baking, throwing ceramics, hiking, and coding for fun. She is passionate about diversity, equity and inclusion of all stakeholders in research.
